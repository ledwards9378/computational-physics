Task 12b

Prism transmission and deflection graphs

Overview

Recreate the prism graphs: θt vs θi, δ vs θi for a fixed apex angle, and a family of δ(θi) curves for multiple apex angles α. BK7 Sellmeier dispersion provides n(λ) at a chosen frequency.

Key Theory

With θi measured to the entry‑face normal and apex α, the emergence angle relative to the exit‑face normal satisfies sin θt = √(n² − sin² θi) sin α − sin θi cos α. The white‑light deflection is δ = θi + θt − α. For a given n the minimum incident angle for transmission (no total internal reflection at the exit) is θi,min = sin⁻¹[n sin(α − sin⁻¹(1/n))]. The code marks this as a red dashed line.
